Privacy Policy

Kufar Price Helper, version 1.1.0

Kufar Price Helper does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Local data

The extension stores only the data needed for its user-facing features in the browser's local extension storage:

  • extension settings;
  • cached exchange rates;
  • saved Kufar listing URLs;
  • listing titles;
  • local BYN/USD price snapshots for saved listings.

These values stay on the user's device. The developer does not receive this data.

Network requests

The extension requests exchange rates from the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus API:

https://api.nbrb.by/exrates/rates?periodicity=0

For saved listings, the extension may request the saved Kufar listing URL in the background to update the local price history. It does not transmit page content, browsing history, form input, account data, or listing details to the developer.

No tracking

The extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, remote code, user accounts, or a developer-operated backend.
