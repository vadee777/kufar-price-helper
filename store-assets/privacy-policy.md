# Privacy Policy Draft

Kufar Price Helper does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The extension stores only local settings, a cached USD exchange rate, selected Kufar listing URLs, listing titles, and local price-history snapshots in the browser's local extension storage. These values stay on the user's device.

The extension makes a network request to the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus API to retrieve the official USD exchange rate:

`https://api.nbrb.by/exrates/rates/431`

For tracked listings, the extension may request the saved Kufar listing URL in the background to update the local price history. It does not transmit page content, browsing history, form input, account data, or listing details to the developer.
